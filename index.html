Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about thousand island. can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to us

What activities can I do in the Thousand Islands?

The Thousand Islands offer a variety of activities, such as snorkeling, diving, island hopping, fishing, kayaking, and relaxing on the beach. You can also explore marine parks, visit historical sites like Onrust Island, and enjoy the local seafood cuisine.

Are there any guided tours available in the Thousand Islands?

Yes, many travel agencies offer guided tours, including island-hopping packages, snorkeling trips, and historical tours to islands like Onrust and Kelor.

Do I need a special permit to visit the islands?

In general, no special permit is required to visit most of the Thousand Islands. However, if you plan to visit conservation areas or conduct activities like research, you may need to obtain permission from the relevant authorities.

Are there accommodations on the islands?

Yes, several islands, such as Pulau Macan, Pulau Sepa, and Pulau Putri, offer accommodations ranging from resorts and cottages to budget guesthouses. You can choose based on your preferences and budget.

What is the best time to visit the Thousand Islands?

The best time to visit is during the dry season, from April to October, when the weather is sunny, and the sea is calmer, making it ideal for outdoor activities. Avoid the rainy season to ensure a more enjoyable trip.
