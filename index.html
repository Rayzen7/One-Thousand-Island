Let's Explore the Wonders of the World

A Hidden Paradise
Near the Capital

The Thousand Islands is a breathtaking escape that blends natural beauty, serenity, and adventure. Just a few hours away from the vibrant energy of Jakarta, this stunning archipelago offers an unforgettable experience for those who cherish the sea and nature.

Destinations on OneThousand Islands

Macan Island

Eco Resort - Tiger Island

The environmentally friendly concept, snorkeling, kayaking and relaxed atmosphere make Eco Resort Pulau Macan the best choice for a romantic honeymoon

IDR 1.000.000 - IDR 1.500.000

Tidung Island

Love Bridge - Tidung Island

The iconic Love Bridge, located on Tidung Island, is a must-visit destination for travelers. Connecting Big Tidung Island and Small Tidung Island

IDR 5.000 - IDR 10.000

Pramuka Island

Mangrove - Pramuka Island

Pramuka Island is famous for its turtle conservation, snorkeling, diving, mangrove trekking, and stunning sunset views from the pier. Nearby Semak Daun Island is ideal for camping.

IDR 50.000 - IDR 150.000

Semak Daun Island

Snorkeling - Semak daun island

Semak Daun Island, an uninhabited gem in the Thousand Islands, is ideal for camping. It boasts white sandy beaches, clear waters, and a tranquil vibe, perfect for snorkeling.

IDR 20.000 - IDR 50.000

Ayer Island

Floating cottages - Ayer island

Ayer Island, part of the Thousand Islands, is known as the "Pearl of the Islands." It features exclusive resorts with floating cottages, modern facilities, and a relaxing tropical atmosphere.

IDR 300.000 - IDR 500.000
